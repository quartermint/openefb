Build the digital logbook: LogbookEntry SwiftData model, auto-population from flight recording data, review/edit/confirm workflow, manual entry creation, chronological list view, and wire recording stop to logbook auto-creation.

Purpose: After a flight recording ends, the pilot gets a pre-filled logbook entry (departure, arrival, duration, aircraft) that they review and confirm. This is the pilot's official flight log -- accurate, editable, and scannable.
